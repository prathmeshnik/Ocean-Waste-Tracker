About Us

Detecting and classifying garbage in water bodies is crucial for effective pollution control and management. Our project, "Water Trash Detection System," is built using advanced object detection technologies like YOLO and CNN. This system aims to identify various types of waste, including plastic, organic waste, and other pollutants, helping improve waste management and reduce water pollution.

Our Mission

To develop innovative technology solutions that help identify and monitor water pollution, enabling more effective cleanup efforts and conservation strategies.

Our Vision

A world where technology plays a crucial role in preserving clean water sources and protecting aquatic ecosystems for future generations.

Our Technology

AI Models

We use state-of-the-art object detection models like YOLO (You Only Look Once) and CNNs (Convolutional Neural Networks) trained on diverse datasets of water pollution images.

Development Stack

Our system is built using Python, Flask, OpenCV, TensorFlow, and modern frontend technologies to provide a seamless user experience.

Data Analysis

We process detection results to generate insightful statistics and visualizations that help understand pollution patterns and trends.

Privacy & Security

We prioritize data security and user privacy in all aspects of our application, ensuring that your information remains protected.
